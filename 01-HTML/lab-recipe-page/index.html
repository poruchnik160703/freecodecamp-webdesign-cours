Russian Borscht

Borscht is a classic Slavic soup, full of flavor and aroma, made with beets, vegetables, and optionally meat. It's perfect for cold days and traditionally served hot with sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium beetroot
  • 1 potato
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 head of cabbage
  • 200g beef or pork (optional)
  • 1 liter of water or broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • Salt, pepper, and 1–2 bay leaves
  • Sour cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. Boil the meat in water until fully cooked (about 1–1.5 hours). Remove it from the broth and cut into small pieces.
  2. While the meat is cooking, wash, peel, and chop the beetroot, potato, carrot, and cabbage.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables to the broth. Cook for 10–15 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. In a pan, sauté the onion and carrot with tomato paste until fragrant. Add this mixture to the soup and cook for another 20–30 minutes.
  5. Add garlic, bay leaves, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir well and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with a spoonful of sour cream on top.
